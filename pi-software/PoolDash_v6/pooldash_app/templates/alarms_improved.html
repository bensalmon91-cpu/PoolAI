ℹ️ Alarm Reference Guide

Severity Levels:

Critical: Immediate attention required - system may not be dosing
Warning: Attention needed soon - may affect water quality
Info: Normal operation status or cleared alarm

Common Alarms:

Manual Mode: Controller set to manual - automatic dosing disabled
Probe Fault: Sensor not responding - clean or replace probe
Pump Fault: Dosing pump issue - check pump and chemical supply
Flow Switch: No water flow detected - check pump operation
Low Chemical: Chemical tank needs refilling

What to do:

  1. Check the "Suggested Action" for each alarm
  2. Critical alarms should be addressed immediately
  3. Warning alarms should be addressed within 24 hours
  4. Info events are for monitoring only
  5. If alarm persists after action, check controller display for error codes
