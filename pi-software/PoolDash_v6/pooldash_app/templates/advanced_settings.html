Data Preservation Policy

All data is kept locally for AI analysis until disk space runs low.

  1. Normal operation: ALL data is preserved at full resolution
  2. Low disk space (<500MB free): Sync to remote server, then aggregate old data
  3. Emergency (<200MB free): Sync to remote, then delete oldest data
  4. Remote sync: Data is ALWAYS uploaded to MOD Projects before any deletion

Your 7GB+ database will be preserved until disk space becomes critical. Configure remote sync to ensure data is backed up before any cleanup occurs.
